My current projects

  • Shift Schedule Algorithm
    • Implment in C++
    • Allows managers to instantly generate shift schedules that would've previously taken hours of work
    • Allows managers to priotize how employees are scheduled based on factors like:
      • Number of unfilled shifts in generated schedule
      • Employee time-off-requests
      • Employee self-set availability
      • Employee employer-set availability
        • this factor will not be displayed to employees by default
        • Cost of schedule--based on employee salary, added cost of any overtime included in the schedule, and any number/combination of cost-multipliers and variable/flat costs which can be customized based on dozens of parameters imported from Schedule Builder and optionally ShiftRank/EmployeeRank
